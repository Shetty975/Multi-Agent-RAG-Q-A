Annual Leave: Employees are entitled to 20 paid leave days per calendar year.

Sick Leave: Up to 10 paid sick days are provided annually with a valid medical certificate.

Public Holidays: The company observes all federal public holidays based on the employee's work location.

Work From Home Policy: Employees may work remotely up to 3 days a week with manager approval.

Unpaid Leave: Requests for unpaid leave must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
